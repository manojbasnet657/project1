About Our Company

About Us

Since 2010 Manage My Vessel has helped companies and individuals with safety, maintenance & personnel management  along with the day-to-day operations of their organization. 

MMV has over 7000 active users and powers  many of the  largest management companies across the world. We work with our partners to develop and improve our system so they can simply manage their yachts safely and effectively.   

We believe there is a better way to do vessel management. Our Mission at Manage My Vessel is to provide the marine industry an environmentally friendly product that drastically  reduces the need  for paper based systems and to promote environmentally sound polices using technology and workflows.  We are excited to simplify all aspects of vessel management for the community though our software.  We do the hard work so you focus on what matters.

Security & Confidentiality

 The privacy and security of your account is very important to us.  Manage My Vessel uses the latest features and technologies to help ensure the security of your data.  If you wish to know more details please email us at support @managemyvessel.com.  We will be happy to provide you the information you seek.
